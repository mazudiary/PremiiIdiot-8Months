The Afternoon That Changed Everything

It was February 14, 2020 Omar Ekushey Boi Mela, a warm afternoon glowing with golden sunlight. The smell of new books, the chatter of people, and the golden dust of the fair filled the air. I was there with friends, wandering aimlessly through the crowded stalls, lost in stories and laughter.

Then, suddenly, everything shifted. Eva’s eyes lit up, and she called out a name that would change my life forever: "Lopa!" In that instant, the world seemed to pause. Amongst the sea of strangers, I found you, the friend I had been looking for without even knowing it. 💕

Among thousands of books and countless paths, fate made sure our orbits collided. What began as a simple reunion between friends quickly became something far more profound, a friendship that would grow into the deepest love, the kind that fills every corner of my heart and soul.
